Are you planning on expanding the game functionality?
Yes, we have a dozen or so different implementations that we would like to add in as different levels, such as the letters in words being scrambled or spreading to increase difficulty, but we are always open to suggestions as well
Are you the first ones to come up with this game idea?
There are several games out there that have the same basic idea and some similar functionalities, but our game is trying to combine all of the parts that we like from those games into 1 awesome game.
