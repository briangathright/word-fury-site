Contribute

Our community is always looking for developers, testers, or just regular players giving us feedback to help improve the game.

Join our Google group to keep track of new feature development or bug fixes!

Fork our code base to work on functionality. Once your development is completed, create a pull request for community review and merge.



Help us find bugs! If you see any bugs during play or development, report them on the issue tracker.



Documentation can always be improved. If you see anything that should be documented better, either from a player or developer perspective, please contribute here.
